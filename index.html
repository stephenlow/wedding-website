How we met


We both swiped right and that's how this love story began. We first met at a Starbucks on Main Street Unionville and Stephen charmingly told me as I walked through the door that he had "took the liberty of ordering me an Earl Grey Tea Latte". I responded that I was glad he was not a crazy farmer and that this did not end up like another episode of MTV's Catfish. My first impression of Stephen was that he laughed too loud and that his choice of plaid was slightly overbearing. However, his dashing smile and charisma soon made me look past it all. That was until he told me he was a huge fan of John Mayer... in which I began to question his taste. Pretty harsh, I know... but little would I know that on our next date he would end up serenading me with a guitar AND a harmonica (which included a hands-free contraption of some sorts ... = dedication) performing John Mayer's rendition of "XO" by BEYONCE. I mean, how can one not be swooned? And swooned I was...


Technology has always been a passion of mine and has been a major infuence in many aspects of my life. Who knew that a simple gesture of swiping right on my mobile phone would lead me to connecting with the love of my life. I felt like Theresa and I immediately clicked over sharing our love for music by sending each other tracks off of our Spotify playlists. Capitalizing on the opportunity, I shared with her an old Youtube clip I had recorded of myself singing the classic John Waite tune "Missing You", in hopes of impressing her. Initially, I thought to myself this may not have been the best move since asking her out on a first date was next to impossible. Time and time again she consistently would ensure that she was too busy to fit me into her jam packed schedule! Finally after enough of my corny jokes, we decided to meet at a local Starbucks. I was thrilled, so I decided to pick out one of my finest pieces of plaid to make sure I gave the absolute best first impression. My first impression of Theresa was nothing short of speechless... Not only was she insanely gorgeous, but she was completely down to earth. Getting to know Theresa more and more over the years, you'll learn that she has the most beautiful heart and she cares so deeply about those around her. She continues to teach me how to be a better person everyday, which is just another testament to her being the amazing teacher that she is!

Traditional Chinese Tea Ceremony 9:45 AM - 10:45 AM

The Chinese tea ceremony is conducted on the day of the wedding and sees the bride and groom serve tea to their parents, in-laws, and other family members. Symbolizing the union of two families, it is a meaningful part of the day, typically taking place inside the couple's respective family homes.

Wedding Ceremony 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Being one of the greatest expressions of love that two people could share for one another, a Catholic wedding binds a man and woman together in the eyes of the Lord, blessing them on their wedding day, and bringing friends and families together in celebration of two souls being bound by God. The Catholic wedding ceremony includes a full mass and communion.
